How it works

  1. Set your working hours.
  2. Enter tasks with a name and estimated time.
  3. Schevo schedules start/end times and order automatically.
  4. Adjust anytime — the plan updates instantly.

Schevo automatically creates your work schedule based on the tasks you enter and your working hours. Just input the task name and estimated time — Schevo calculates start and end times, arranges the order, and adapts instantly if things change. Stay on track, finish on time, and reduce the stress of managing your schedule. Whether your day is steady or unpredictable, Schevo helps you focus on getting things done.
